About MyTUT Events

Connecting TUT Students Through Events

The official event management platform for Tshwane University of Technology, bringing together students, faculty, and the community.

Our Mission

To revolutionize campus life at TUT by providing a seamless, user-friendly platform that connects students with meaningful events, fostering community engagement, personal growth, and academic excellence across all campuses.

Community Building

Bringing students together through shared experiences

Academic Support

Enhancing learning through educational events

Innovation

Using technology to improve student experiences

Our Vision

To be the leading digital hub for campus life in South Africa, where every TUT student has access to enriching events that support their academic journey, personal development, and professional growth.

By 2030, we envision:

100% of TUT students actively engaged through our platform
Seamless integration across all TUT campuses
AI-powered personalized event recommendations
Partnership with industry leaders for career events

Our Story

How MyTUT Events came to life

2023 - The Idea

Born from student frustration with fragmented event information across TUT campuses.

2024 - Development

A dedicated team of TUT students and faculty collaborated to build the platform.

2025 - Launch

MyTUT Events officially launches, connecting students across all campuses.

Why We Started

"We noticed students were missing out on amazing opportunities simply because they didn't know about them. Events were scattered across different platforms, making it hard to discover what was happening on campus."
